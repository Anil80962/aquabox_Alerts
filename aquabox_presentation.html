The Problem

Industries face critical challenges in water management
  • Industries waste water due to lack of real-time monitoring
  • Delayed alerts lead to overflow, leaks, and abnormal usage
  • Manual tracking is error-prone and slow
  • No multilingual support for diverse workforce across plant sites

The Solution — AquaBox

An always-on smart display for water infrastructure
  • Raspberry Pi 4 powered 5-inch touchscreen display
  • Real-time water alerts from AquaGen cloud platform
  • Multi-language voice announcements in 6 Indian languages
  • AI-powered water assistant (AquaGPT) for interactive queries
  • Always-on, wall-mounted at industrial plant sites
